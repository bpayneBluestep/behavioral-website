Our Story

We started where our clients are: inside residential treatment facilities. Before the platform existed, we worked the floor shifts, navigated the audits, and filed the documentation our clients manage today. Fifteen years of it. We didn't study this industry. We lived it.

Brandon came up at Havenwood Academy, a residential treatment center in Utah, working as facility staff. The tools available didn't match how adolescent programs operated, so he built his own on the BlueStep platform. One person solving his own facility's problems became the behavioral health division he now leads.

Roy brought the same perspective to the architecture, building it around real clinical workflows: shift-change handoffs, medication administration, compliance documentation. That's the thread across our team. When we build a feature, we're solving a problem we've had ourselves.

We didn't come to behavioral health from a software company. We came to software from behavioral health.

— Brandon, Executive Director
