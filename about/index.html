Our Story

We started where our clients are — inside residential treatment facilities. Before BlueStep's behavioral health platform existed, our team was working the same floor shifts, navigating the same compliance requirements, and filing the same documentation that our clients manage today. Collectively, we have 15+years inside adolescent behavioral health programs. We didn't study this industry from the outside. We lived it.

Brandon came up at Havenwood Academy, a residential treatment center in Utah, where he worked as facility staff. The tools available at the time didn't match how adolescent programs actually operated — so he started building his own on the BlueStep platform. What began as one person solving his own facility's problems became the foundation of the behavioral health division he now leads as Executive Director.

Roy brought the same behavioral health perspective to the technical side — building system architecture around real clinical workflows like shift-change handoffs, medication administration, and compliance documentation rather than retrofitting a generic EHR to fit. That is the common thread across our team: we have been inside the programs we serve. When we build a feature, we are solving a problem we have had ourselves.

We didn't come to behavioral health from a software company. We came to software from behavioral health.

— Brandon, Executive Director
