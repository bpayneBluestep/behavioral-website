The Reality

We know — because we lived it too.

Lagged operational visibility

By the time the spreadsheet is compiled, the data is a week old. You're managing a 40-bed program on last week's information — census shifts, incident trends, staffing gaps. Real operational decisions need real-time data.

Outcomes reporting burden

Payers want measurable outcomes. Accreditors want quality metrics. The state wants incident aggregation. Your team is exporting data into three different spreadsheets and stitching it together manually — for every reporting cycle.

Compliance reporting gaps

You know your program is compliant. Can you prove it to a surveyor in 15 minutes? Can you show trending data — not just a binder of forms? Most programs can't. That gap becomes a finding.

Census and revenue opacity

How many beds are open tonight? What's your average length of stay this quarter? If the answer requires a phone call to admissions and a spreadsheet from billing, that's the problem — not the data itself.
