The Reality

We know — because we lived it too.

Census pressure

Eight empty beds in a 40-bed program isn't a pipeline problem. It's a revenue crisis that starts compounding tonight. Census visibility needs to be real-time — not a phone call to the charge nurse at 5pm.

Lost referrals

Referral comes in by phone. Gets noted on a sticky. Staff member is out the next day. Family calls back — nobody knows the history, nobody can pick up where the last conversation left off. That family finds another facility.

Referral source blindness

Which therapists are sending you patients? Which hospitals? Which school districts? If you can't answer that in 30 seconds, your outreach strategy is a guess. And guesses don't fill beds.

Generic CRM mismatch

Salesforce and HubSpot were built for closing deals. Behavioral health admissions is inquiry to screening to authorization to admission — a pipeline with different stages, different language, and different stakes. Forcing a sales tool into this workflow creates gaps.
