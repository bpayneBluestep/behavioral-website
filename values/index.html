Our Values

Before anything else, we strive to see people as people.

Everything else follows from that. Five values — each one making the next one possible.

01See People→02Truth Before Comfort→03Self-Accountability→04Laser Focused→05Growth with Purpose

Before anything else, we strive to see people as people and value their humanity. When we do so successfully, we can lean on our relational bank accounts to choose truth over comfort. Once our relationships have seeded, we take greater pride in our work and hold ourselves accountable to high standards. Our choices to be relational, transparent, and hold ourselves to high standards means we must be laser focused on our mission; avoiding any distractions which take us away from it.

01

See People

We are a behavioral healthcare company first, and a software business second.

We are a human first organization. We strive to see people as people, and not objects. In the age of artificial intelligence, we value human connection. All our products, services, strategies, experiences, and efforts are to serve our purpose of helping other humans lead happy and fulfilling lives.

  • Relationships require face to face time. We eagerly try to meet our clients in person when available to us and always turn our cameras on.
  • Personal client friendships. Hugs as greetings, gifts for milestones.
  • Critical feedback is always looked at as an opportunity for growth rather than taken as personal offense.
02

Truth Before Comfort

While others exaggerate product capabilities or timelines, we choose honesty and transparency.

We turn business away if they don't fit our niche. We choose to speak candidly as a team, even when doing so is hard or puts us in a vulnerable position. We tell our clients openly if we think they could be doing something better, and they do the same for us.

03

Self-Accountability

We don't have to hold each other accountable; we are accountable to ourselves.

We are our own greatest critics, and we have an inherent desire to excel in everything we commit ourselves to. We openly take accountability for our mistakes, and we move on from them quickly. Carrots and sticks rarely motivate us, but leaving an impact is intoxicating.

  • We don't wait for our manager to tell us what isn't going well. We bring it up openly knowing we have more work to do to turn it around.
04

Laser Focused

We are not for the many, but for the few.

We are hyper focused on building value for our niche in order to create 1,000 true fans. We avoid distractions at all costs. We reduce quantity so that we can excel in quality.

  • Collaborate closely with clients to stay close to the heart of the problems we can help them solve.
  • We prefer sustainable, deep relationships over high churn — we measure success by retention.
  • Celebrate client success through testimonials, case studies, and public recognition.
05

Growth with Purpose

We grow with great intention.

We know that some things require urgency and speed while others call for thoroughness and thoughtful design. We choose slower growth to protect our high client experience standards.

Seeing people as people, choosing truth over comfort, being self-accountable, and staying laser focused inevitably leads to great growth — we choose to be purposeful with that growth, and never sacrificing quality along the way.
