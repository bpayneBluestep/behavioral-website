Outcomes

Surveys across the care journey. Analytics that show what's working.

Proof, not anecdotes

Payers want outcomes. Now you have them.

Reimbursement is moving toward measurable outcomes, and most programs measure by anecdote. Outcomes schedules the surveys, delivers them wherever families are, and turns the responses into data tied to the clinical record. No separate research database. No chasing.

Survey scheduling

Set the schedule once: intake, mid-treatment, discharge, post-discharge follow-up. Surveys go out on time for every patient, automatically. Nobody keeps a spreadsheet of who's due.

Multi-channel delivery

Families and former residents don't answer the same way. Surveys reach them through the channel they'll actually respond to, and reminders follow up so response rates hold after discharge.

Analytics

Responses roll up into outcome trends by program, by cohort, and over time. The data lives with the clinical record, so what you show a payer or an accreditor is the same data your clinicians work from.
